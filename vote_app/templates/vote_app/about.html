About the Voting Project

Welcome to the Voting Project, an Online Voting System designed to provide a secure and user-friendly platform for online voting. Registered users can create and manage elections, join and vote in elections, and view election results once voting ends.

The system ensures the integrity and anonymity of votes, supporting a straightforward process for users to participate in elections securely.
